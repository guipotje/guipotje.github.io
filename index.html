As a science lover, I'm curious and always looking forward to improve and innovate. My main research topic is Computer Vision (more specifically, image correspondence and local image descriptors). I am also interested in related fields such as Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. I obtained my M.Sc. degree in Computer Science from the University Federal of Minas Gerais, working on 3D reconstruction and image registration.
